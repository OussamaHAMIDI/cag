Who are we ?

Welcome


CAG Business Service

is a subsidiary of CAG Law Firm, and is dedicated to any services that businesses could need. We have a team of attorneys backed by chartered accountants, consultants in management, strategy, legal and administrative services, industrial and intellectual property, accounting, finance, taxation… which are available at future and current entrepreneurs’ convenience.

Our role consists in guiding and accompanying businesses, particularly startups, throughout the launch phase (searching for funds, legal procedures, administrative, accounting and taxation procedures, business structure, establishing the strategy…)

Moreover, we will be fully engaged in accompanying the business in its future steps of its development.

Services

the following list contains the services provided by CAG Business Services

SERVICES

Searching for Funds via CAG’s network

Searching for funds is a crucial step towards the business realization. We have our own approach via which we help entrepreneurs find the financial resources that are necessary. This is done thanks to our network composed of investment funds, banks, SICAR, business angels, European funds, funds from the Golf region and North America.

Strategic Assistance

We offer our clients a strategic orientation in order to help them better develop a vision for their business as well as provide the necessary coaching to strengthen their skills.


The Creation of the Startup

During this step, we will be in charge of writing the legal status along with all the other administrative steps: the API registration, authorisations, specifications, registration of trademarks at the INORPI, deposition of patents, protection of the intellectual property on a global scale.

Taking care of the accounting & the bank account

A clear accounting, updated regularly, is essential to the vitality of a company and the visibility of the financial information. However it requires time and energy, same goes for the bank account management. This is why we propose to take care of this as part of our financial services with the help of our team in order to assure a reliable and updated financial information.




Training Services

We offer our clients different professional and multidisciplinary trainings according to their needs. Our trainings are being held by academic professors from different universities (ISG, IHEC, MSB, INSAT) as well as renowned coaches.
*The services are provided by a coaching office approved by the CNFCPP

Assistance in Writing the Business Plan

Knowing the importance of a business plan in the creation of a business, and all along its existence, we propose this service with the aim of guiding our clients as well as participate in the creation of the business plan. The purpose is to give a realistic image of the project as well as optimizing the chance of its realisation.

Pay, Income Tax Return and CNSS

We guarantee to our clients a complete assistance in the process of submitting the income tax return and the CNSS, in conformity with the requirements as well as the imposed deadlines.

Administrative Assistance

We ensure to take care of the domiciliation, billing, document management, assistance of bureau d’ordre, classification of documents according to the bank account.



Legal Assistance

Trademark registration, patents, protection of intellectual property on a global scale, management of the legal case (meeting minutes, management reports…), writing all legal documents: acts between partners (shareholders' agreements, increase in capital, shares transfers) but also, the contracts necessary for the client’s activity (employment contracts, service contracts, leasing, maintenance contracts, purchase agreements …etc.) and finally represent you in court.
*These legal services are provided by CAG Law Firm, which is registered under the Tunisian Lawyers Council

Transport and Transit

Our transport service consists in providing our clients with a courier service prepared to fulfill all the needs: from transporting personnel to transporting documents and goods. Our transit service is also provided by a customs authorized forwarder.
